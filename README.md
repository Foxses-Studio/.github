## 🚀 Our Vision
To build a **powerful yet simple business operating system** where companies don’t need dozens of disconnected tools. Foxses aims to become a unified alternative to platforms like Zoho, HubSpot, and GoHighLevel — but **simpler, faster, and more affordable**.

---

## 🧩 Our Ecosystem
Foxses is built as a modular collection of integrated products under one platform. Everything is connected, yet functions independently.

### 🏢 Core Products
* **Foxses CRM** – Manage leads, customers, pipelines, and sales.
* **Foxses Commerce (Fcomers)** – Build and manage online stores with ease.

### 📈 Marketing & Growth
* **Foxses Marketing** – Email campaigns and automation.
* **Foxses Forms** – Collect leads and customer data.
* **Foxses LandingPage** – High-converting landing page builder.
* **Foxses Social** – Social media management tools.

### 💬 Communication & Engagement
* **Foxses Mail** – Multi-domain email sending and routing system.
* **Foxses Chat** – Live chat for websites.
* **Foxses Booking** – Appointment scheduling.

### ⚙️ Business Operations
* **Foxses Inventory** – Inventory and stock management.
* **Foxses Helpdesk** – Customer support system.
* **Foxses FSM** – Field service management tools.

### 🛠️ Productivity & Utilities
* **Foxses Sign** – Digital signature solution.
* **Foxses Domains** – Domain management and DNS tools.
